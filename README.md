# Coding project #1 - Combat-Training-Tracker

## Description: 
Combat-Training-tracker is a web app, aimed to target users who train all types of combat training sports (MMA, wrestling, BJJ, etc.) (still need to think of a better title, but this will have to do), that allows you to track your training, lifting progress, nutrition, and body weight. 

## Main Features: 
The main features will include a sign-up/in page and 4 screens: a home screen, a training screen, a nutrition screen, and a calendar screen. 

## Tech stack:
* ## Frontend  - HTML/CSS, JavaScript
* ## Backend- Java (springboot) 
* ## Database- MySQL

## Screen features:
* Sign in/up screen: Pretty self-explanatory, a page where you can sign into the app, and if you don't have an account, you can sign up using a usable email. 
* Home screen: Home screen will contain things such as your current training streak, a bar showing your current weight, and a reminder of the type of training you are doing for that day
* Calendar screen: This screen will help you schedule your training & lifts within the day, especially when the athlete is in HS/college, trying to balance training & classwork.
* Training & performance screen: This screen will be able to log your lifts (reps & ranges for each muscle) as well as your training and the things you worked on, how you can improve on them (like journaling your practices and reflecting on them) 
* Nutrition screen: This screen will be used to log your daily food intake for breakfast, lunch & dinner, while also tracking daily macros, such as your current protein and carb intake
